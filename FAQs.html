Frequently Asked Questions

What flavors of cakes do you offer?
We offer a wide variety of flavors including vanilla, chocolate, red velvet, carrot, lemon drizzle, and seasonal fruit flavors. Custom flavors are also available upon request.
How far in advance should I place my order?
We recommend placing your order at least 5-7 days in advance to ensure availability and customization options, especially for large or custom cakes.
Do you offer delivery services?
Yes, we offer delivery within our service area. Delivery fees may apply depending on the distance. Please contact us for details and scheduling.
Can I customize the designs for my cake?
Absolutely! We specialize in custom cake designs to match your theme and preferences. Simply let us know your ideas and we will work with you to create a beautiful cake.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and online payments for your convenience.
